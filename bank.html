Specialized Bank Debt Recovery Services

At DigiBhoomi, we offer end-to-end debt collection services specifically designed for banks and NBFCs. Our recovery processes are structured, compliant, and rooted in years of experience dealing with loan defaults, credit card dues, and non-performing assets (NPAs).

We bridge the gap between institutions and defaulters through timely communication, field engagement, and legal follow-through. Our priority is always to protect your financial reputation while ensuring efficient and ethical recovery.

Our Service Includes:

  • Early delinquency follow-ups to avoid NPA classification
  • Recovery of personal loans, credit cards, vehicle & home loans
  • Field agents for on-ground visit and collection
  • Borrower profiling, tracing, and KYC validation
  • Handling of high-ticket and legal recovery cases

Who We Serve:

  • Banks with unsecured & secured loan defaults
  • NBFCs facing high delinquencies in consumer loans
  • Microfinance institutions requiring field-level recovery
  • Loan aggregators needing specialized third-party recovery

DigiBhoomi combines regulatory knowledge, professional manpower, and modern tracking systems to deliver reliable recovery results. With zero tolerance for harassment and 100% compliance, we are your trusted collection partner in the financial ecosystem.
